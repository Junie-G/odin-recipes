Banana Bread Recipe

Grannie's family famous banana bread!

Nearly everyone loves the taste of banana bread! I almost always have a loaf of fresh, moist banana bread sitting on the dining room table for the family to snack on between meals, and I think itâ€™s a good habit others should adopt too.

The best bananas for banana bread are the ones that are already black, or at least dark brown. To keep banana bread moist in storage, be sure to keep it in an airtight container like plastic wrap or Tupperware! You can also freeze banana bread for four months and it will stay good.

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 1/2 tsp vanilla
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F. Grease metal loaf pan with nonstick cooking spray.
  2. Mash banana with a whisk. Mix in sugar.
  3. Mix in melted butter.
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together the milk and the egg.
  5. Add the milk mixture, vanilla, baking soda, and salt to banana bowl and mix well.
  6. Fold in the flour and the chocolate chips with a spatula until batter forms.
  7. Our batter into loaf pan and bake for 45 minutes or until a toothpick is inserted in the middle and comes out clean.
