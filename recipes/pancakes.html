Grannie's fluffy pancakes!

These flapjack pancakes are quick, easy, and turn out fluffy every time.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup canola oil
  • cooking spray
