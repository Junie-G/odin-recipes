Pancake Recipes

Grannie's fluffy pancakes!

These flapjack pancakes are quick, easy, and turn out fluffy every time.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup canola oil
  • cooking spray

Steps

  1. Whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a bowl until no lumps remain.
  2. Add milk, eggs, oil; whisk until batter is runny and smooth.
  3. Heat a nonstick griddle over medium heat and lightly coat with cooking spray.
  4. Laddle a 1/4 cup batter for each pancake onto preheated grill and cook until bubbles appear, 3-4 minutes. Flip and cook, pressing middle gently with spatula, until bottom is lightl browned, 2-3 minutes. Repeat with remaining batter.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
